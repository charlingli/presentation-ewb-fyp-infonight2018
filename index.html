My FYP

Scenario

Financing and administration issues are systemic in Higher Education Institutes in Cambodia (Chealy, 2009).

  1. Inappropriate use of the scarce resources in Cambodia
  2. Poorly matched demand at the labour market for skills valued in the workforce
  3. Little regard for academic freedom and integrity

My FYP

Motivation

To foster a generation of well-rounded graduates through introducing internship programs to better train STEAM students in soft skills. To deliver to the reader a set of guidelines (and in lieu of evidence, considerations) to support/develop internship programs.

My FYP

Deliverables

Anatomy of an Internship.

  1. Goals and Objectives
  2. Selection of Organisations
  3. Selection of Candidates
  4. Project Initiation and Monitoring
  5. Impact and Sustainability

My FYP

Findings

Gender Equality

  • Entrenchment of gender roles
  • Low employment prospects
  • Personal safety and security
  • Discrimination from peers, lack of support

Recommendations

  • Small-scale mentoring assistance
  • Female-targeting scholarships

My FYP

Findings

Socioeconomic Equality

  • Stigma against 'poor' people
  • Financial constraints

Recommendations

  • Evening sessions to target night students
